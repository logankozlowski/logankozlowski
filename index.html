Problem statement: Student Transportation

The transportation provided by the university or lack thereof, is not enough for the everyday college student. The busses either aren't on time, being too early or too late, or their stops are in inconvenient spots where the 20-minute walk would be faster.

Group Members: Logan Kozlowski Ryan Evans Chad Heath Aidan Ehlers

Affinity diagram: Student Transportation

For our affinity diagram regarding transportation for students on campus, we covered 5 major categories. Those being E-bikes, E-scooters, an application to track those E-devices, student bus transportation, and charging stations for any electric transportation devices that may be necessary.
